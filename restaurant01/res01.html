Our Menu

Yummy Tacos

Made with real taco ingredients, you'll love our amazing taco food. This food is hand-crafted in our kitchen and microwaved using a real microwave. If you need more microwaves, just ask!

Water

If you are thirsty, you are in for a treat. Our water is made with real hydrogen and oxygen, lovingly mixed using an ancient recipe. We serve all our water in a cup - you don't have to use your hands.

Live Music

Some say that the harmonica is the fax machine of the soul. If you need a quick pick-me-up, come on in and listen to the soothing harmonica ballads offered by our own Chef Carl.
