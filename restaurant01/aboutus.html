About Us

Who We Are

We are a group of semi-motivated food makers. We like food, sort of, and we think you might like food too. We started Yum Yum Foo with a few dollars in our pockets and it was a Tuesday, I think. We all are very proud of ourselves, I can tell you that. Even on the rainiest day, we can smile into the wind and breathe with our hearts and paint with the colors of the future.

We founded our restaurant with one goal in mind: deliver good food to good people in all walks of life, and play music while they eat, and tell a story or two so that they leave happy and full and ready to face the day with wide arms and light hearts. Also we needed to make some money and this seemed like a good idea. I'm not really sure what else to write here.

Our Team

Bob Bee Senior Greeter

Bob has been greeting people for many years, and you will agree that he is the best greeter. There are few greeters that meet the level of greetyness that Bob does. If you happen to stop in and Bob gives you a great big smile and says 'Welcome to Yum Yum Foo!', then you know you are at Yum Yum Foo. Because this is where he works.

RoboVac 4000 Robot Vacuum Cleaner

How do we keep our floors so clean? You might want to ask RoboVac 4000, or 'RV'. Sometimes there might be a piece of food that has fallen on the floor that is too big for RV. Maybe a child dropped it, or maybe it rolled there and nobody is going to blame you, just tell me so I don't slip on it and fall, OK? Basic manners, people. Really.

Some Guy Coffee Drinker

This guy is always here. We asked his name but he just kind of grunts. But that doesn't mean he isn't friendly, he's just realy focused on his Sudoku puzzle. You see, you match up all the numbers in the boxes like this, and when they all fit then you win. Its a big deal and lots of smart people play Sudoku.

Maya Something Waitress

Maya is the only one who actually does any work around here. She made this website and is always going the extra mile. She cleans the restaurant after it closes, manages the finances and plans the menu. She really is an amazing woman and a true friend.
